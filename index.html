Outdoor Coffee Roasting Experience

Roast Your Own Coffee with Traditional Tools

Description

Immerse yourself in the art of coffee roasting with a hands-on experience that takes you back to the basics. Using just a cast iron skillet, wooden spoons, and a camping stove, you'll learn how to roast your own coffee beans in a beautiful outdoor setting. This unique adventure is perfect for anyone looking to connect with nature, learn a new skill, and enjoy the rich flavors of freshly roasted coffee.

What You'll Do

  • Select Your Beans: Choose from a variety of green coffee beans, each with distinct flavor notes and origins.
  • Roast on a Camping Stove: Roast your own beans using a cast iron skillet over a camping stove under expert guidance.
  • Savor the Fresh Brew: Cool, grind, and immediately brew your freshly roasted beans.
  • Take Home Your Beans: Package your roasted beans in a rustic mason jar to take home.
  • Relax and Enjoy: Conclude with a relaxed coffee tasting session in natural surroundings.
